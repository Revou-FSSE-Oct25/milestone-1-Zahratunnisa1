Tentang Saya

My name is Zahratunnisa. I graduated in Chemical Engineering in 2022 from Mulawarman University in Samarinda. I have worked for approximately three years in a swasta company in Balikpapan as a laboratory analyst. In the midst of my career, I discovered a new passion in the IT world.

I was initially amazed at how the world, especially daily life, has become easier because the presence of great software on our mobile phones, start from e-banking, e-commerce, online motorcycle taxis, cashless payments like Q-RIS, and many more. I wondered how to create such a great application like that. I really wanted to have the ability to create applications that could simplify our daily lives. I searched for ways, studied on my own through the internet, but it felt like it wasn't enough. Until finally, in middle of 2025, I discovered Revo-U and decided to take the FSSE 2025 to deepen my knowledge.
